Other form elements

Form Label

Usage: As a single element where the syle of the default form label is needed.

Modifiers:

  • .form-label--light
    Lighter gray variation of the form label

  • .form-label--marg-b10
    Adds a bottom margin of 10 pixels.

  • .form-label--block
    The form label has inline-block as the default display property. This modifier changes it to block.
